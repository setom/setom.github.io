Map my (Browser) History

There is no cloud, there is only someone else's computer. When you browse through the Internet you are making calls to those computers. But where are they? Just clicking through a few websites could bring you thousands of miles across oceans and contints. We collected our own browser history for approximately a week and pinged their IPs and server locations.

Where Did I Go Today?

This map shows the results of locations of those servers. Unbeknownst to us, we all hit servers outside of North America. Click the map points to discover site names and locations, there are some surprising results!
