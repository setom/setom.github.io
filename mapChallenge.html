The Heartbeat of the City

22 May, 2018 in Traffic

Day 1: Points

Ottawa, ON funds outdoor rinks in the winter for public use. I pulled and geocoded rink addresses and presented them using QGis overlaid with a streets layer to help orientation.

These maps are 1-hour contributions to the 30 Day Map Challenge to keep my skills fresh and try/learn new techniques
