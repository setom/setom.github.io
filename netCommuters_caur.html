Visualizing Commuting Data

Note: the interactive version of this visualization is not available for public viewing, please contact me for further details

Using Mapbox GL on our LAMP stack we visualized the commuting habits of residents in the south Puget Sound Region. This data represents the net daily commuters in and out of an area each workday. We can see that the downtown Tacoma core swells with workers from outlying suburban areas.
