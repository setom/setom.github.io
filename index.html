About Me

Originally from Ontario, Canada, I now make my home in Washington State. I am a Master of Science student at the University of Washington-Tacoma

I completed an undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Systems and worked in industry at a Seattle-area startup as product owner for an enterprise-grade, multi-platform file sharing service. In this role I honed my skills as a fierce customer advocate, technical spec-writer, and UI/UX designer for web and mobile software products. When I'm not working or completing my research, you can find me in the mountains.
